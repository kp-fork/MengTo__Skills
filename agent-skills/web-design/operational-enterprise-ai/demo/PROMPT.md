# Operational Enterprise AI Demo Prompts

## Minimal prompt

Use $operational-enterprise-ai to create an original enterprise automation page that makes permissions, approvals, audit evidence, exceptions, and rollback as visible as the product benefit.

## Recreate the demo

Use $operational-enterprise-ai to recreate the design quality and behavior of the included Relay Systems demo as a complete standalone `index.html`.

Build a near-black cinematic shell with hairline grid lines, warm-white type, muted gray labels, tabular numerals, and one restrained pale signal color. Place a boundary-led headline and qualified actions on the left. On the right, show a clearly labeled sample workflow trace with intake, policy, prepared action, audit packet, human approval gate, checks recorded, pending approval, and rollback target.

Use square panels, low radii, minimal shadow, and no glowing orbs, particle clouds, or cyberpunk decoration. Every operational module should expose summary, permissions, action, approval, output, audit, exception, and rollback. Build semantic solution rows that expand with Enter or Space and retain a text equivalent for any diagram.

Add loading, unavailable integration, insufficient permission, stale data, denied approval, partial completion, rollback, network error, and success states. Any metric, customer, badge, or compliance claim must be verified or removed. Keep the page responsive, keyboard accessible, legible at 200% zoom, and immediately settled under reduced motion. Use only local HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and fictional sample data.

## Remix prompt

Use $operational-enterprise-ai and the included demo as the quality bar for a different controlled automation product. Replace the brand, workflow, claims, metrics, labels, and conversion context. Preserve the visible system boundary, approval gate, audit and rollback model, dark operational hierarchy, semantic disclosures, responsive layout, and reduced-motion support.
